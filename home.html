Functions

Are you looking to hold a party or work function?
Black Fire can cater to all your needs with our private room, complete with its own bar, projector, and retractable screen.

The private room can comfortably fit up to 30 guests for your event and has a $1500 minimum spend (Sun-Thu). Guests will be treated to one of our delectable banquet menu options.

On Friday and Saturday nights, the private room is available for two sittings (max. 2 hrs) for a minimum spend of $1500, or spend the whole night for $2500 (min. spend, max. 4 hrs).

For parties larger than 30 guests, we can offer two of our rooms, with a private bar and standing space, for a minimum spend of $3000.
